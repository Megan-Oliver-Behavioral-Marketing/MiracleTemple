Our Story

A House Built on Faith, Love & Purpose

For over three decades, Miracle Temple has stood as a beacon of hope in our community — a place where the broken find healing, the lost find direction, and the faithful find family.

Under the leadership of Senior Pastor Emmanuel & Pastor Grace Osei, we are committed to building disciples who carry the love of God into every corner of the world.

Our Ministries

Prayer Ministry

Intercession is our foundation. Our prayer warriors meet daily to stand in the gap for our congregation and community.

Children's Ministry

Raising the next generation in the Word. Dynamic, engaging programs for children from infants through age 12.

Youth & Young Adults

A vibrant community for teens and young professionals hungry to encounter God and connect with purpose.

Praise & Worship

Our worship team leads the congregation into the presence of God through anointed music and Spirit-filled praise.

Community Outreach

We are the hands and feet of Jesus — feeding the hungry, clothing the needy, and serving our city.

Bible Study

Deep, transformative study of God's Word in small groups designed to ground believers in Scripture and doctrine.
