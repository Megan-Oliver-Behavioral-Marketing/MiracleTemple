Children's Ministry

Raising the next generation in the Word, one heart at a time

Led by Monique Oliver, daughter of Pastor Terence Oliver

Our Heart

Nurturing Kids in the Word

Miracle Temple's Children's Ministry exists to plant the love of God deep in the hearts of our youngest members here in Byhalia, Mississippi. Through Sunday School, worship, and hands-on activities, children learn that their faith is real, personal, and something to be excited about — not just something they hear about, but something they get to live out.

Every child who walks through our doors is welcomed, known by name, and given a safe, joyful place to grow — right here in our Byhalia community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages does the Children's Ministry serve?

We welcome children from infants through age 12, with age-appropriate teaching and activities for every stage.

When does Children's Ministry meet?

We meet every Sunday at 10:00 AM during Sunday School, right here at Miracle Temple in Byhalia, MS.

Who leads the Children's Ministry?

Monique Oliver, daughter of Pastor Terence Oliver, leads our Children's Ministry with a heart for raising up the next generation in the Word.

Do I need to register my child in advance?

No advance registration is needed — simply bring your child with you on Sunday morning, and our team will help get them checked in and settled.

What should my child wear?

Come as you are! We ask that clothing be modest and comfortable enough for kids to sit, sing, and move around freely during activities — no need to dress up.
