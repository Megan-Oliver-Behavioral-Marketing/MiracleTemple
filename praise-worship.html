Praise & Worship

Leading the congregation into God's presence through anointed music and praise

Our Heart

Ushering In His Presence

Our Praise & Worship ministry exists to lead the congregation of Miracle Temple in Byhalia, Mississippi into the presence of God through anointed music and Spirit-filled praise. Whether through song, instrumentation, or heartfelt worship, our team's goal is simple: to point every heart in the room toward Jesus.

We believe worship isn't a performance — it's an offering. Every voice, every instrument, and every note is given as a gift back to the One who gave us everything.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join the Praise & Worship team?

Yes! We welcome anyone with a heart for worship, whether you sing, play an instrument, or simply want to serve behind the scenes. Reach out through our contact page to get connected.

Do I need to audition?

We ask that new team members meet with our worship leadership first, simply to find the best fit for your gifts and for the team — it's more of a conversation than a formal audition.

What instruments or vocal parts are needed?

We welcome vocalists of all ranges, as well as musicians on keys, drums, guitar, bass, and more. If you have a gift, there's likely a place for it on the team.

When does the team rehearse?

Rehearsal schedules are shared directly with team members. Contact us to learn the current schedule and get plugged in.
