Who We Are

A House Built on Faith, Love & Purpose

Miracle Temple is a Spirit-filled house of worship led by Pastor Elder Terence Oliver and First Lady Marieta Oliver. Together, they have shepherded this congregation since July 18, 2021, building a community rooted in faith, love, and purpose. Miracle Temple stands under the jurisdiction of Mississippi Northwest, and we are committed to reaching our community with the Gospel, discipling believers, and walking alongside every person who calls this house home — whether they've been with us for years or are just now finding their way in.
